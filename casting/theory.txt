What do you mean by upcasting and downcasting?
Upcasting is when you take an object and cast it to its superclass type or parent type.
downcasting is when you take an object and cast it to its sub types or child class types.
Eg: Suppose there is Animal class with a method makenoise it prints animals make noise and a child class dog which overrides the makenoise method by printing woof woof and an additional method growl which prints grr Upcasting would be taking a dog object and casting it to animal class and downcasting would be taking an animal object and casting it into dog type.
